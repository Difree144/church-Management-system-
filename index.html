Who We Are

A Place of Faith,
Hope & Community

Church of God in Nambale, Matunda is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community committed to worshipping God, growing in faith, and serving the people of Busia County and beyond.

  • Worship — We honour God through authentic, heartfelt praise and prayer every Sunday.
  • Word — Our teaching is grounded in Scripture, relevant, and life-transforming.
  • Community — Every person is valued, known, and cared for in our church family.
  • Mission — We are called to reach our community and the world with the Gospel.
